Welcome to ABC, your number one source for all things furniture,
We're dedicated to giving you the very best of furniture, with a focus on dependability,
customer service and uniqueness.
Founded in year 1996 by Mr. Sarath Gunasekara, ABC has come a long way from it's
beginnings in a garage. When Mr. Sarath first started out,
his passion for furniture drove him to quit his job and focus on his passion and gave
him the impetus to turn hard work into a booming online store.
We now serve customers all over Sri Lanka, and are thrilled to be a part of eco friendly
and fair trade wing of the furniture industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have
any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
Sunith Gunasekara, CEO.
